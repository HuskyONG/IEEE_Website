About
Who We Are

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization, dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE publishes nearly one third of technical papers and research all across the realm of electronics.

IEEE actively develops thousands of industry standards, including some you use everyday ("Wi-Fi" is actually IEEE standard 802.11!). Internationally, IEEE represents the absolute forefront in electrical engineering, computer science, and anything in-between.

Why Join?
Hands-On Projects

Work on real robots. Build hardware. Write firmware. No toy problems, we build things that compete.

Skill-Building Workshops

Free workshops every semester covering soldering, CAD, ROS, PCB design, Python, and more.

A Great Community

Collaborate with engineers across every discipline. Build friendships and professional connections that last beyond graduation.

Open to Everyone

No experience needed. We actively train new members, all majors and all years are welcome to join.

How to Donate

Thank you for considering a donation to IEEE @ UIC! Below are instructions for donations online or by check.

Online Donations
Donate Online
Visit: https://payment.give.uic.edu/campaigns/48413/donations/new
Fill out the giving form. In the Designation dialog box, select the option "other" and then type UIC IEEE Fund.
Select the payment method and complete the transaction.
Receive recognition on our website and in team materials.
For additional giving instructions or help, you can visit https://advance.uic.edu/ways-to-give-info/
Donations Via Check
Donate by Check
Make a check payable to University of Illinois Foundation.
In the Memo section, make sure to include: "UIC IEEE Fund".
Mail your donation check to:
University of Illinois Foundation
P.O. Box 734500, Chicago, IL, 60673-4500
Receive recognition on our website and in team materials.
For additional giving instructions or help, you can visit https://advance.uic.edu/ways-to-give-info/
